I recently completed a web assignment given by NAMI College, where I created a website related to motovlogging and bikes. The website has a simple yet modern design, with easy navigation and functionality. To add a touch of creativity to the website, I used icons from boxicons.com to enhance the visual appeal of the website.

The website features an introduction to motovlogging and its significance in the world of biking, along with a list of popular motovloggers that users can follow. The website also contains a section for reviews of bike accessories and parts, where users can post their feedback and share their experiences with other bike enthusiasts.

I made sure that the website is optimized for search engines, so it can easily be found by anyone looking for information related to motovlogging or bike reviews. Additionally, the website is mobile-responsive, which means it can be accessed easily on smartphones and tablets.

I thoroughly enjoyed working on this project, as it gave me the opportunity to combine my passion for biking and web development. It was a challenging task, but with determination and hard work, I was able to deliver a high-quality website that met all the requirements of the assignment.

Overall, I am proud of what I have accomplished, and I hope that the website will be useful for anyone interested in motovlogging and bikes
